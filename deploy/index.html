Work

What I need:

To get started on a project I'll need a few things:

  • Photographs

    Photographs of the project / property. The more photos you provide, the better.

  • Address

    Address of the property. Sometimes it's helpful to look at satellite photos of the house.

  • Drawing / Sketch

    A drawing/sketch of your idea for the project. A list of materials your client wants to use. Please include measurements on all objects in your drawing.

What you get:

Three renderings from three different angles (Primary, Secondary & Top View). One line drawing with dimensions. These measurements are for estimation purposes only and are not certified dimensional numbers.

Each rendering will be in a templated format that has your logo. I will also use your logo as a watermark over the renderings.
